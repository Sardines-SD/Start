Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Municipal Service Portal, we collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, ward number, and account preferences.
  • Service Requests: Descriptions, categories, images, and status updates of issues you report.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the portal to help us improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us:

  • Process and track your service requests efficiently.
  • Communicate updates about your requests.
  • Improve municipal services based on community feedback.
  • Ensure accountability and transparency in service delivery.

3. Data Protection

We take data security seriously. Your information is stored securely using Firebase's industry-standard encryption. Only authorized municipal personnel have access to your data on a need-to-know basis.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is shared only with:

  • Municipal workers assigned to your service requests.
  • Government auditors for performance monitoring.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Significant changes will be notified via email or a notice on the portal.
